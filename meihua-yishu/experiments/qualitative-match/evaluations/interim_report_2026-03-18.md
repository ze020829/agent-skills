## Standout Predictions

### Real Hexagram Dominates (diff ≥ +3)

**E02 — Lunar Eclipse (Real=5, Control=1)**
Real hexagram 賁→明夷 (Adornment → Darkening of Light): "Fire illuminating mountain at night... brilliance reaching peak then withdrawing into darkness." This is a near-perfect description of a blood moon eclipse — dramatic light-and-shadow, peak brightness giving way to darkening.

**E11 — SOTU Aftermath (Real=4, Control=1)**
Real hexagram 井+睽 mutual (The Well + Opposition): "Two people at the same well, backs to each other... the clearer the offering, the more sharply divided the response." Precisely captures the longest-ever SOTU generating maximally polarized partisan reactions.

**E17 — Pixar Hoppers (Real=4, Control=1)**
Real hexagram 噬嗑→晉 (Biting Through → Progress): "Biting through obstruction... sunrise after difficult night." Captures Pixar's rebound after years of theatrical disappointments, with Hoppers becoming their best original hit since Coco.

**E18 — NVIDIA GTC (Real=5, Control=1)**
Real hexagram 火天大有 (Great Possession): "Fire blazing above heaven, sun at zenith, leader commanding vast resources illuminating an entire industry." Jensen Huang's $1 trillion revenue projection and sweeping announcements matched this imagery perfectly.

### Control Dominates (diff ≤ -2)

**E01 — Apple Launch (Real=2, Control=4)**
Control hexagram's "emperor bestowing gifts" and "ceremonial abundance" better captured the 6-product launch than the real hexagram.

**E04 — Tariff Challenge (Real=2, Control=4)**
Control's "gentle penetration through cracks" better described the legal battle (24 state AGs filing lawsuits) than the real hexagram's overly positive tone.

**E14 — Colombia Election (Real=2, Control=4)**
Control's "institutional decay and cauldron breaking" better captured the divided, no-majority congress.
