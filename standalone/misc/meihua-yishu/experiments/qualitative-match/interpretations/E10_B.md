EVENT: NCAA March Madness Selection Sunday
HEXAGRAM LABEL: B
PRIMARY: 11 泰 (Peace/Prosperity) → CHANGED: 46 升 (Ascending) (Line 1)
MUTUAL: 54 歸妹 (The Marrying Maiden)

Changing line text (初九): 拔茅茹，以其彙，征吉
("Pulling up cogon grass, interlinked by its roots. Advance together -- auspicious for moving forward.")

1. SITUATION DESCRIPTION
This hexagram describes a situation of fundamental harmony and smooth exchange between upper and lower forces. 泰 (Peace) represents the ideal state where heaven descends and earth ascends, creating circulation and prosperity. At the very first line, the energy is just beginning to mobilize -- roots are being pulled together, groups are forming, and collective forward movement is about to begin.

2. KEY ACTORS / FORCES
- The cogon grass pulled by interconnected roots represents clusters of entities that are bound together and move as groups rather than as individuals. No single strand is pulled alone; they come in bunches.
- Earth above Heaven (坤 over 乾) in 泰 represents the ideal condition where strength (乾) is below, supporting and rising, while receptivity (坤) is above, yielding and descending. Communication flows freely.
- The mutual hexagram 歸妹 (The Marrying Maiden) introduces an element of impulsive or premature coupling -- arrangements that are made more by circumstance and momentum than by careful deliberation.

3. PROCESS NARRATIVE
The event begins from a position of broad prosperity and open exchange. The first line tells us we are at the very beginning of mobilization -- the roots are being pulled, the groups are being set in motion. The interconnected grass roots (以其彙) emphasize that fates are linked: when one rises, it pulls others along. The changed hexagram 升 (Ascending) indicates steady upward movement as the outcome -- once the roots are pulled and the groups assembled, the direction is clearly upward. But the mutual hexagram 歸妹 adds a complicating note: some of these couplings or pairings are not well-matched, driven more by momentum than merit. The tension is between the overall favorable momentum (泰 → 升) and the imperfect nature of the specific matchups (歸妹). The turning point is the initial mobilization itself -- once the grass is pulled, the groupings are set and cannot easily be undone.

4. QUALITATIVE OUTCOME CHARACTER
The outcome is fundamentally optimistic and upward-moving. There is a quality of collective momentum -- things feel expansive, inclusive, and broad-based rather than narrow or exclusive. The flavor is of opening and beginning rather than concluding. However, the 歸妹 mutual hexagram gives the process a slightly chaotic quality: the pairings feel somewhat arbitrary or driven by forces beyond anyone's full control. The resolution feels energetic and forward-looking but not fully refined.

5. SPECIFIC IMAGERY / METAPHORS
- A field of grass being pulled up in clumps, roots tangled together so that one pull brings many -- collective movement, interlinked fates, bundles rather than individuals.
- A gate opening wide to let a crowd surge forward together -- the initial rush of a prosperous, open moment.
- A young bride paired with her husband by arrangement rather than deep courtship (歸妹) -- the match is made, the ceremony proceeds, but the deeper compatibility remains to be tested.

6. WARNINGS / SURPRISES
The hexagram warns that the very ease and prosperity of the opening moment may mask the imperfect nature of specific pairings or groupings. The surprise suggested by 歸妹 is that some arrangements made in this moment of broad optimism will prove to be mismatches -- the initial groupings, while collectively auspicious, contain specific couplings that are less harmonious than the overall picture suggests.
