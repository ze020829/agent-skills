EVENT: Cygnus NG-24 ISS Resupply Launch (Apr 9)
HEXAGRAM LABEL: A
PRIMARY: 13 天火同人 (Tong Ren / Fellowship) -> CHANGED: 37 風火家人 (Jia Ren / The Family) (Line 4)
体用: 體=離(火), 用=乾(金). 體克用, 體方有利.

1. SITUATION DESCRIPTION
Fire beneath heaven: the image of Fellowship, where a single flame illuminates the open sky and brings together those who share a common purpose. "同人于野，亨" -- fellowship in the open field is prosperous. The structure depicts a collaborative endeavor where diverse forces unite toward a shared objective. Fire (離) overcomes metal (乾) in the five-element cycle, meaning the luminous, energizing principle represented by 體 actively transforms the rigid, structural element of 用. The situation favors the animating force -- the spark, the mission, the driving purpose -- over mere institutional weight.

2. KEY ACTORS / FORCES
The fourth line reads: "乘其墉，弗克攻，吉" -- mounting the city wall but unable to attack, and finding this auspicious. This image captures a moment of reaching a critical threshold only to discover that restraint, not assault, produces the favorable outcome. The transformation into Family (家人) speaks to the conversion of outward alliance into inward order -- "利女貞" -- the discipline of the household, where each member fulfills their role within a structured whole.

3. PROCESS NARRATIVE
The narrative opens with a broad coalition assembled for a shared purpose, fire reaching toward the heavens in unified aspiration. The fourth line introduces the critical moment: the endeavor reaches its wall -- a boundary, a technical threshold, a point of maximum tension. At this juncture, the hexagram counsels that the inability to force through is itself the blessing. The transformation into Family suggests that the resolution comes not from breaking through barriers but from tightening internal coordination, ensuring every component is properly aligned. Fire's dominance over metal means the mission's energy reshapes the material infrastructure to serve its purpose.

4. QUALITATIVE OUTCOME CHARACTER
The outcome has the quality of a well-coordinated collective effort that achieves its aim through discipline rather than brute force. It feels like a campfire that draws all members into its circle -- warm, ordered, purposeful. The final state is domestic in character: things properly in their places, roles clearly defined, the satisfaction of a well-run operation.

5. SPECIFIC IMAGERY / METAPHORS
- Fire ascending to meet heaven -- aspiration rising to the highest point, visible from afar.
- Mounting a wall without attacking -- reaching the critical boundary and finding success in restraint.
- An open field narrowing to a family hearth -- broad alliance condensing into tight operational order.
- "先號咷而後笑" (from line 5) -- first weeping, then laughing: initial anxiety giving way to joy.

6. WARNINGS / SURPRISES
The surprise lies in line four's paradox of success through apparent failure to breach. Whatever wall the endeavor encounters, the hexagram says the inability to attack through it is fortunate -- suggesting that a pause, a recalibration, or a moment of restraint at the critical juncture is what enables the positive transformation. The warning is against factional fellowship ("同人于宗，吝") -- success requires inclusive coordination, not cliquish narrowness. The transformation into Family reinforces that order and role clarity, not individual heroics, determine the final outcome.
